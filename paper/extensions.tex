\chapter{Extensions}
\label{cha:extensions}

\textit{ \begin{itemize}
    \item Detail the assumptions made in constructing the results
    \begin{itemize}
      \item Rational expectations
      \item Simultaneous decision making
      \item Revenues are unimportant to the regulator
      \item Monopoly markets
      \item Markovian strategies
      \item Emissions flow rather than stock regulation
      \item Linear taxation scheme
    \end{itemize}
    \item Discuss the consequences of relaxing the
    assumption. Particularly, extend the model to include
    \begin{itemize}
      \item Sequential decisions
      \item Government concern about revenue gathering
      \item Duopoly market
      \end{itemize}
  \end{itemize}
}
